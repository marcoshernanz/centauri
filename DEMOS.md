# DEMOS.md

## Demo 1: Hacker News Top 5 Summary

### Goal
Show fast, human-like navigation across multiple pages and grounded summarization.

### Prompt
`Open Hacker News and summarize the top 5 posts. For each one, include the main takeaway and why it matters.`

## Demo 2: Gmail Inbox Triage + Auto-Draft Replies

### Goal
Show autonomous action execution with input filling (reply drafting), labeling, and mailbox cleanup.

### Prompt
`Open Gmail, review my last 5 unread emails, draft replies for the 2 most urgent ones, apply the label "Follow Up", and archive low-priority newsletters. Do not send anything.`
